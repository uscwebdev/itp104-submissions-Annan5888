Rice Noodle Soup

Wenzhou pig intestines rice noodle soup (Chinese: 温州猪脏粉) is a noodle soup dish made with rice noodles, pig intestine and duck or pig blood. It comes from Wenzhou, in Zhejiang, China. It can be eaten at breakfast or throughout the day.Intestines are cooked in aromatics such as rice wine, soy sauce, ginger, soybean paste, or garlic until softened.

Fish Cake

Fish cake is a Wenzhou special snack. Taking fresh sea fishes such as brown croaker and mackerel from the East China Sea as the main raw materials and matching with unique seasonings, it is crafted by adopting traditional recipes and advanced techniques. Wenzhou fish cake is characteristic of its tender texture, delicate flavor, not-fishy taste, low-in-fat, and rich-in-nutrient.

Fish Ball

Wenzhou Fish Ball is a traditional famous food in Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province. As snack food commonly seen in water-town households, Wenzhou Fish Ball is different from fish balls in other regions. The soup is clear, slightly sour and hot. The fish balls are elastic and mostly irregularly long, which are translucent jade white.
